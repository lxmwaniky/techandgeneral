What are the chances ?

~Ghost of tsushima -The Game.

There is no denying it, technology has crept into our day to day lives a lot, especially these days with the pandemic grounding most of us. And with that internet usage is on an all-time high, teleconferencing, online tutoring and offline and online video gaming #PUBG #FORTNITE have seen an over 40% increase in active monthly users. RPG franchises like Ghost of tsushima and assassins creed (which are my all time favoutites) shall be covered.
