The Top 10 podcasts for techies

~ by Techies

Top 10 podcast for techies.

I like podcasts, especially how ambient they can get. This enables me to multi-task or even listen during a commute. In fact, I was listening to the votegasm 2020 mini-series within the daily show as I wrote this list. It doesn't matter which app or platform you listen on though I recommend Spotify and Google podcasts and Apple podcasts for iPhone users. The title states 10 best podcasts for techies, not 10 tech podcasts, as a heads up not all podcasts on the list are foundationally technological but appeal to many 'techies'.

With that said let's get into it, shall we? We shall start with the tenth talk show and work our way up the list.

10.Ted Talks Tech

Inevitably, the guys that produce and host one of the best video shows on technology have an awesome podcasts for us.TED talks are one of the most influential figures in the technology scene, with every ted talker leaving us intrigued and inquisitive. Making us ask ourselves the right questions.
Join the conversation now by searching for it on your favorite podcasting app.
My favorite episode on TED talks Tech - "What you need to know about stalkerware | Eva Galperin a new frontier in cybersecurity and cyberbullying" a hefty 13 minutes read-only.

9.The vergecast

My news feed is full of the verge. I go to The Verge for almost all my tech updates, since they have such an authority in the space. Almost all tech news breaks on this online publication's widely popular website, so when I heard they heard they hosted a tech podcast I scrambled. This pod is for those who, like me, can't afford to stay abreast of the ever-changing world of Tech. Artillery for my next tech-related squabble with my peers
. Even the podcast's description lures me" Hello! This is The Vergecast, the flagship podcast of The Verge... and your life. Every Friday, Nilay Patel and Dieter Bohn make sense of the week's tech news with help from our wide-ranging staff. And on Tuesdays, Nilay hosts in-depth, one-on-one interviews with major technology leaders. Join us every week for a fun, deeply nerdy, often off-the-rails conversation about what's happening now (and next) in technology and gadgets."
Check em' out, search "THE VERGECAST" on your favorite podcast platform and get on top.

8.The WAN Show

FACT, did you know that "The WAN Show" stands for "The Weekly Analysis & News Show". I have successfully binged almost 4 hours of Linus Tech Tips and Techquickie on YouTube in one sitting. Linus is arguably the most influential tech YouTuber out there. From his main YT channels he gurners a staggering 1 billion views. The show is hosted by Linus and Luke from LTT. The show is fun, interactive, and very informative. What sets aside the WAN show is that it features tech updates almost immediately before a week has barely passed. It's great for bleeding edgers. I Can't point out a single episode, they are all really fun LOL.
Listen on Spotify Listen on Podcasts Or check it out on your favorite podcast platform

7.Winner Winner Chicken Dinner


This is for my fellow PUBG die-hards that can't get enough of it on the fields of Erangel spraying bullets and snipping heads off:-). Listen to deep insightful discussions on the game. Tactics, strategies, and best practices are all covered here. The talk show also discusses the game's general development, release schedule, and bugs.
